Pizza

Description

There's nothing quite like a slice of hot, gooey pizza straight from the oven. This classic Italian dish is a favorite around the world, and with so many topping options, there's a pizza for everyone.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1 cup diced onions
  • 1 cup sliced olives
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the pizza dough into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza pan.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella and parmesan cheeses over the sauce.
