Lasagna

Description

This classic Italian dish is made with layers of pasta, meat or vegetables, and cheese, all baked together in a delicious tomato sauce. It's the perfect comfort food for a cold winter's day.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 9 lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large saucepan, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the beef is browned and the onion is translucent. Drain off any excess fat.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce
