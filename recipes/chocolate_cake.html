Chocolate Cake

Description

This rich and indulgent chocolate cake is sure to satisfy any chocolate craving. It's moist, fluffy, and full of chocolate flavor, and is perfect for any special occasion or just a sweet treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup hot coffee
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate mixing bowl, whisk together the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, hot coffee, and vanilla extract.
